# WHY I Built This Project

## The Canadian Business Problem
Every Canadian company needs to send announcements instantly.
Manual email takes hours. This SNS system delivers in seconds.

## Real Canadian Use Cases
- RBC: Send fraud alerts to 15 million customers instantly
- City of Toronto: Emergency notifications to residents
- Shopify merchants: Flash sale notifications
- Federal Government: Policy announcements to stakeholders

## Nokia Connection
At Nokia I worked with pub/sub messaging for 5G network alarms.
AWS SNS is the same pattern. I already understood this architecture.
I just needed to learn the AWS names.
